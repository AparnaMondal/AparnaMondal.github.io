About Me

I'm Aparna Mondal, a Senior Systems Software Engineer with 19+ years of experience riding tech transformations — from embedded telecom systems to cloud-native infrastructure and now AI-driven engineering.

My journey spans the full depth of the tech stack — building high-performance protocol stacks for LTE, 3G, WiMAX and 5G NR, delivering post-silicon validation frameworks at Intel, and architecting cloud-native NFV solutions demonstrated at MWC Barcelona.

Today I'm at the intersection of deep systems engineering and AI — exploring agentic frameworks, LLM-assisted development and production-scale ML systems, while sharing my learnings one post at a time.
